Xiaomi Himo C26

Electric bike

Full freedom of movement and reliable transport for travel over any distance

HIMO C26 is a new electric bike from Xiaomi, which is designed for both urban and off-road riding. It has a powerful motor and a high-capacity battery, thanks to which it is able to travel up to 100 km on a single charge, which is definitely higher than most competitors. And large wheels with pneumatic tires, disc brakes, a 7-speed transmission and an ergonomic seat make every ride much more enjoyable.

Comfort and safety from HIMO

HIMO C26 enables you to get to your place of work or study quickly and comfortably without being tied to city transport. The bike has a sturdy construction and an impressive range to be a reliable means of transportation in today's urban environment. Lightweight and durable frame, 26-inch wheels and 7-speed transmission system allows you to move confidently not only on the asphalted roadway, but also on a difficult suburban track.

Comfortable saddle

HIMO C26 e-bike is equipped with a comfortable saddle by the famous brand Selle Royal. The three-dimensional gel structure of the saddle provides optimum elasticity and high comfort during the ride.

Combination of modes to increase power reserve

The HIMO C26 electric bike has an extended range. Its 10,000 mAh battery with intelligent power optimization allows up to 60 km on a single charge in electric drive mode. With the use of physical force on some sections of the road, the range is increased to 100 km. The maximum driving speed of the HIMO C26 is 25 km/h.

Reliable transmission

The 7-speed transmission system delivers smooth, precise shifts, comfortable speed control and reliable performance. Shift through the gears to get you up and down the hill with minimal effort.

All information on the LCD display

The large, informative display on the steering column displays all important information about the HIMO C26 in real time. You will be able to see the current speed, the level of charge or the distance traveled. The display is IPX7 protected and not affected by rain or dust.
